# HKGD Demon List — Submission Guidelines

Welcome to the HKGD Demon List (HKGDL) Guidelines! This document explains how to submit records, what we accept, and what we don't.

---

## 1. Eligibility

- You must have a valid HKGDL account at https://v2.hkgdl.dpdns.org/#/account
- You must claim your player profile before submitting a record
- You must be a member of the HKGD (Hong Kong Geometry Dash) community
- Admins reserve the right to remove accounts that are not legitimate HKGD members

### Claiming Your Profile

After creating an account, you **must** claim your player profile to sync your demons from the HKGD Spreadsheet to HKGDL.

1. Go to your account page and enter your registered HKGD Spreadsheet name
2. Wait for your claim to be accepted by an admin
3. Only **one** player name can be bound per account

> If your claim is pending for 48 hours, contact an admin on Discord.

## 2. Record Requirements

TL:DR : FOLLOW THE AREDL REQUIRMENTS.IF YOUR VIDEO IS ALREADY ACCEPTED IN AREDL THEN IGNORE EVERYTHING BELOW :D

### 2.1 Video

- You **must** record and upload a video of your completion to a supported video platform
- The video must show the **entire completion** from 0% to 100%
- The video must be publicly accessible and not deleted after acceptance
- We recommend using [OBS](https://obsproject.com/) to record your screen, microphone, and game audio

### 2.2 Clicks

- **All clicks must be clearly audible** throughout the entire completion in both your public video and raw footage
- Clicks from a physical mouse or keyboard are the **only** accepted click sounds
- **Clickbots, CPS counters, and input viewers are NOT substitutes** for real clicks and are banned
- If your clicks are very quiet, your record may be denied as reviewers need to distinguish them from the level's song
- You may remove microphone audio from your public video **only if** you submit raw footage with audible clicks

### 2.3 End Screen Stats

- Your record **must show the end screen stats** (attempts, stars, orbs, jumps, time)
- If you've completed the level before, you **must** disclose this in the submission notes
- We recommend using a mod to "Uncomplete" the level before rebeating it (to avoid suspicion from zero stats)

### 2.4 Cheat Indicators

- If you use any mod or hack with a cheat indicator, **all cheat indicators must be clearly visible** on the level's end screen
- The cheat indicator must show that no cheats were used during the completion
- If you use a mod menu (e.g. Mega Hack, Eclipse, Prism Menu), the cheat indicator on the end screen must be enabled
- If you do not use any mod or hack that has a cheat indicator, this rule does not apply to you

### 2.5 Versions

- If you are playing on a version of Geometry Dash that is not the latest, you must follow the guidelines for that version
- For pre-2.2 completions: FPS counter must be visible, and framerate must not exceed 360 FPS
- GDPS records are treated as the GD version the GDPS is based on

## 3. Raw Footage

If your record is in the **top 100** on HKGDL, you must submit raw footage along with your public video.

### What is Raw Footage?

- An unedited recording of your completion straight from your recording software
- You must **not** edit this file in any capacity before submitting
- Must be at least 30 FPS
- Must include your clicks on a **separated and isolated audio track**

### How to Record Isolated Clicks

Your microphone audio (clicks) must be on a separate audio track from game audio:

- **OBS**: Set up multiple audio tracks — one for game audio, one for microphone only. See [this guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YNlRUVk3oE8)
- **Medal.tv**: Use multi-track recording. See [this guide](https://support.medal.tv/support/solutions/articles/48001220541-multi-track-recording)
- **GeForce Experience**: See [this guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IP-3dJw0uMY)

If you cannot record with isolated audio tracks, you may record your clicks separately with another device and upload both files.

### How to Submit Raw Footage

- Upload to a file-sharing platform (e.g. Google Drive) that allows **anyone with the link** to download the file
- The file must **not** be modified/compressed by the platform
- The link must not expire
- YouTube is **not** accepted for raw footage (it strips audio tracks)

### Raw Footage Confidentiality

Raw footage is confidential to the list team and will not be shared with anyone outside the team. You may delete raw footage after your record is reviewed and accepted.

## 4. Modding & Custom Copies

### 4.1 Disallowed Mods

- You may **not** use any mod that gives an unfair advantage (e.g. speed hacks, auto-clickers, macro mods)
- Clickbots are banned in all records, even if your completion also has real clicks
- Toggling hacks during an attempt to change the difficulty is banned

### 4.2 Custom Copies

If you are using a custom copy of a level (e.g. LDM, bugfix), it must:

- Be the published version on the servers (not a locally stored copy)
- **Not** modify the gameplay of the level
- **Not** remove enough detail to make the level easier
- **Not** add or change decoration of gameplay sections

Custom copies may be approved by list staff. Contact an admin on Discord with the level ID and a video showcase to request approval.

### 4.3 Skips

During your completion, you may **not** use any skips that make any section of the level significantly easier. Skips that barely affect the difficulty or make a section harder are allowed. If you are unsure about a skip, contact a list moderator before submitting.

---

## 5. Two Player & Co-op

- Unless stated otherwise, all completions must be performed by a single player
- A second player may only participate in sections that use the in-game 2-player mode
- If you complete a Two Player level solo, you must include a clearly visible webcam showing both hands throughout the entire completion
- Players may not switch who performs single-player sections within a single attempt
- Remote completions via Globed or Parsec are allowed for Two Player levels

### Globed 2P

- Globed 2P completions require both POVs in the video or a link to the other POV
- Use of Deathlink 2-player copies is allowed for levels that don't work with Globed's built-in 2P mode
- The copy must retain the dual physics from normal dual sections

---

## 6. Alt Accounts

Submitting records with alternate accounts is **not allowed** under any circumstances. This includes:

- Ban evasion
- Impersonation
- Submitting records for another player

You may only submit **your own records** under your main account. Violations will result in the alt account being deleted and possible temporary/permanent ban from the list.

If your video is on a platform not listed here, contact an admin to request support.

---

## 8. Level Statistics

- The level must be rated in Geometry Dash to be on HKGDL
- Level difficulty, objects, and other stats are synced automatically from GD servers
- Video URLs are optional — if provided, they must be valid links to a completion video
- Level descriptions and song info are pulled from GD servers when available

---

## 9. Record Statuses

When you submit a record, it will be in one of the following states:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Waiting for admin review |
| **Accepted** | Record approved and displayed on the list |
| **Rejected** | Record denied — you will receive a reason |
| **Under Review** | Record is being evaluated by staff |

If your record is rejected, you may fix the issues and resubmit.
